# Dark Matter Suit / 暗物质太空服

**One suit. Every need gone. Fly, dig, build, and conquer.**
**一件太空服,摆脱一切需求。飞行、速挖、纵横全图。**

A handful of sand condenses the ultimate spacesuit. Wear it, and a duplicant transcends survival entirely — no breathing, no eating, no sleeping, no limits. Every feature can be toggled in the in-game mod config.

一捧沙子凝聚出终极太空服。穿上它,复制人彻底摆脱生存 —— 不呼吸、不进食、不睡眠、无极限。所有功能都能在游戏内配置面板单独开关。

---

## Features / 功能

**Survival / 维生**
- Infinite oxygen & fuel — never suffocates / 无限氧气与燃料,永不窒息
- Freezes stamina, calories, bladder & stress / 冻结体力、卡路里、膀胱、压力
- No sleep needed — works around the clock / 不需睡眠,全天候工作
- Full environment protection — magma, cold, radiation, debuffs / 全环境免疫:岩浆、严寒、辐射、各类 debuff
- Invincible — takes no damage at all / 免伤,完全无敌
- Disease & germ immunity / 免疫疾病与病菌
- Slow health regeneration / 缓慢回血
- **Bionic duplicant support** (battery/oxygen/oil/gunk handled) / **适配仿生复制人**(电池/氧气/机油/淤泥全包)

**Ability / 能力**
- +1000 to all 12 attributes (adjustable up to 10000) / 全 12 项属性 +1000(可调至 10000)
- All skills unlocked — no morale penalty / 全技能解锁,不增士气期望
- Never removed at suit checkpoints / 永不在检查点被脱下

**Movement / 移动**
- Flight through air and vacuum / 空中与真空飞行
- Fly through liquids / 水中飞行
- Adjustable flight speed / 飞行速度可调

**Digging / 挖掘**
- Insane dig speed from maxed attributes / 满属性带来的极速挖掘
- Chain digging — clears whole marked areas / 连锁挖掘,整片标记区一扫而空

**Appearance / 外观**
- Standard spacesuit silhouette with a dark-matter purple tint / 标准太空服造型 + 暗物质紫色

---

## How to get / 获取方式

Craft at the **Crafting Station** with just 1 kg of sand — practically free.

在**工艺站**用 1 kg 沙子制造,几乎零成本。

## Config / 配置

Open **Options** on the mod entry. Every feature is a toggle or slider; UI auto-switches between English and Chinese by game language.

点 mod 条目的 **Options**。每个功能都是开关或滑条;界面按游戏语言自动中/英切换。

## Compatibility / 兼容性

- Base Game & Spaced Out! / 本体与眼冒金星 DLC
- PLib bundled in — no extra dependency / 已内置 PLib,无需额外依赖
